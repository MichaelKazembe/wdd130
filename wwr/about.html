Nyasa Rafting

Nyasa Rafting is a Malawian company that offers thrilling rafting adventures on the Shire River. Our team of experienced guides will take you on an unforgettable journey through the stunning landscapes of Malawi. Whether you are an experienced rafter or a first-timer, we have the perfect trip for you.

History

Nyasa Rafting was founded in 2010 to bring thrilling river adventures to the heart of Malawi. Inspired by the beauty of the Shire River, the company set out to offer safe and memorable rafting experiences. Starting with a small, passionate team, Nyasa Rafting quickly became known for its professionalism and commitment to adventure.

Over the years, it expanded to include kayaking, camping, and eco-friendly tours, attracting enthusiasts from around the world. The company also partners with local communities to promote sustainable tourism and environmental conservation.
